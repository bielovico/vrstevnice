Visualization of data from exhibit Interactive sandbox in VIDA! science centre.

Here you can find an overview of all data gathered from an exhibit. Hue and saturation of a color is defined by an active mode and brightness denotes interactions with the exhibit. Black color means there is no activity at the exhibit, that means it is either off (during the weekend mornings) or there are no people interacting with it.

Click on a specific date to open a new window with interactive view.

By clicking and dragging you can select an area for a full detail view. Double click on the area opens a new window with detailed view. Single click outside of the area clears the sleection. You can move and/or resize the slection.
